Now what?

Welcome! So now what? I started this website as a way to reach out more, but I had another motive for this, and that is so I can write more. I have created a blog space to write about a variety of experiences, from travelling, painting processes, and seeing artist work, but I also want to delve back into my love for art history and express this here too.

I recently got to have the opportunity to feature on Jo’s Art History Podcast talking about Marina Abramovic and Yoko Ono, two artists who have been highly influential to me. Marina Abramovic is known as a pioneer for female performance artists, the godmother of performance art she is often referred to as. Yoko Ono on the other hand is in my opinion an underrated artist who is only really known for her association with The Beatles, yet she deserves so much more credit as an artist.

The Yoko Ono podcast episode will be released very soon!

I hope this website gives me the literary outlet I desire, and more importantly provides an accessible way to see what I am up to as an artist, where I am currently showcasing work, and also have people reach out, I would love to talk to other people about art things, and even take a commission if you feel so inclined to!

Here is to the future, let’s see what happens next.
